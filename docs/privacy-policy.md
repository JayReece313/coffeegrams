# CoffeeGrams — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 18, 2026
**Developer:** JR Labs LLC

## The short version

**CoffeeGrams does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information.** There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers,
and no third-party SDKs.

## What data the app handles

- **Your brew log** (the brews you save, along with any ratings and notes) is
  stored **only on your device**, using Apple's on-device database (SwiftData).
  It never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else.
- **Purchases.** The one-time "CoffeeGrams Pro" purchase is processed entirely by
  **Apple** through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment details.
- **Notifications.** Cold-brew and French-press reminders are **local
  notifications** scheduled on your device. They are not sent through any server.
- **Diagnostics.** The app uses Apple's on-device **MetricKit** to record crash
  and performance information locally. This stays on your device; we do not
  receive it unless you separately choose to share diagnostics with developers
  through Apple's own settings.

## What we do NOT do

- We do **not** collect personal data.
- We do **not** use analytics or advertising.
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.
- We do **not** use any third-party SDKs.

## Children

CoffeeGrams is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including
children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
