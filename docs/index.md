**CoffeeGrams** is a precise, privacy-first coffee brewing companion for iOS —
dose-and-ratio calculators and guided timers for six brew methods (V60, Chemex,
French Press, AeroPress, Cold Brew, and Espresso).

- [Privacy Policy]({{ '/privacy/' | relative_url }})
- [Support &amp; FAQ]({{ '/support/' | relative_url }})

CoffeeGrams collects no data — no accounts, no tracking, no ads. Everything stays
on your device.
