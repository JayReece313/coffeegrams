# CoffeeGrams — Support

Need help with CoffeeGrams? We're happy to help.

**Contact:** info@jrlabapps.com

We aim to reply within a few business days.

## Frequently asked questions

**What's free?**
French Press is completely free, with all its features — the calculator, the
guided brew timer, and saving brews to your log.

**What does CoffeeGrams Pro unlock?**
A one-time **$4.99** purchase unlocks the other five methods — V60, Chemex,
AeroPress, Cold Brew, and Espresso. No subscription; it's yours forever.

**I bought Pro but it's locked on a new phone / after reinstalling.**
Open any locked method to bring up the Pro screen and tap **Restore Purchase**.
Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to buy it.

**Where is my brew data stored?**
Entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded. (This also means brews don't yet
sync between devices — iCloud sync is planned for a future update.)

**My brew reminders aren't showing up.**
Reminders use local notifications. Enable them in **Settings → Notifications →
CoffeeGrams**, then start a cold brew or French press again.

**How do the timers work?**
Pick a method, set your dose and ratio, tap **Start Brew**, and the app guides
you through each pour/steep step with a countdown. Espresso shows a shot timer
that turns green inside the 25–30s target window.
